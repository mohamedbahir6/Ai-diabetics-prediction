About Diabetes

What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic disease where the body cannot properly control blood sugar levels because of insufficient insulin production or ineffective insulin usage.

Common Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow wound healing

Risk Factors

  • Obesity
  • Family history
  • High blood pressure
  • Physical inactivity
  • Age above 45

Prevention Tips

  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat healthy food
  • Maintain healthy weight
  • Avoid smoking
  • Regular health checkups
